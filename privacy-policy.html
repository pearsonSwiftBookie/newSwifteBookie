Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2024

Introduction

At SwiftBookie, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our booking platform and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our platform, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business details necessary for creating and managing your SwiftBookie account.
  • Booking Information: Data related to bookings, scheduling, and reservations made through our platform.
  • Usage Information: Details about how you interact with our platform, services, and features.
  • Device Information: Information about the device and internet connection you use to access our platform, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our booking platform and services
  • Process and manage bookings and reservations
  • Communicate with you about your account, services, or support needs
  • Send you important service updates and administrative messages
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures designed to protect your personal information from accidental loss and unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

SwiftBookie will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, regulatory, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
  • The right to data portability

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
